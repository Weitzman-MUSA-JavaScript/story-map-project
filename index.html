Philadelphia Ethnic Food Clusters

Moving off of the University of Pennsylvania's campus during the Covid-19 pandemic, I found myself in a drastically different food environment.

In my new neighborhood of Spruce Hill, Philadelphia, I discovered the succulent chicken sandwiches at Saad's Halal Restaurant, the tender lamb leg at Hadramout, and discovered a wealth of other middle eastern and halal eateries.

As a spatial data enthusiast, this led me to wonder how the food landscape of Philadelphia varies across geographies.

This storymap documents what I've found through a year of research and data collection from Yelp's Fusion API and the Encyclopedia of Greater Philadelphia.

Philadelphia Restaurants, according to Yelp
